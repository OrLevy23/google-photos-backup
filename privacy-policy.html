Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Google Photos Backup application ("the App") handles your data. The App is a personal backup tool that allows you to download and store your Google Photos locally on your device.

2. Data Collection and Use

The App requests access to your Google Photos library with read-only permissions. The App:

  • Only accesses your photos and videos that you have stored in Google Photos
  • Downloads your media files to your local device for backup purposes
  • Does not upload, modify, or delete any content from your Google Photos account
  • Does not share your photos or data with any third parties
  • Does not collect or store any personal information beyond what is necessary for the backup functionality

3. Data Storage

All downloaded photos and videos are stored locally on your device in a directory you specify. The App does not transmit or store your data on any external servers.

4. OAuth Authentication

The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. This allows secure access to your Google Photos library. Your Google account credentials are never stored by the App - only an access token is used for API requests.

5. Permissions

The App requires the following Google Photos API scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/photoslibrary.readonly - Read-only access to view and download your Google Photos library

This scope allows the App to read your photos and videos but does not permit modification or deletion of any content.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Google Photos Library API provided by Google. Your use of this API is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the App's access to your Google Photos at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Delete any locally stored backups created by the App
  • Stop using the App at any time

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the app developer through your preferred communication channel.

10. Open Source

This App is provided as-is for personal backup use. The source code and functionality are transparent, allowing you to verify how your data is handled.
