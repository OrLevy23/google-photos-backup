Google Photos Backup

This is a personal backup tool that allows you to download and store your Google Photos locally on your device.

Purpose

The application provides a way to create local backups of your Google Photos library, giving you full control over your photo collection and ensuring you have a copy stored on your own device.

Features

  • Download all photos and videos from Google Photos in full/original quality
  • Organize files by date automatically
  • Incremental sync - only downloads new or changed files
  • Read-only access - never modifies or deletes your cloud photos

Privacy

Your photos are downloaded directly to your local device. The application does not store, transmit, or share your data with any third parties.

For more information about how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
